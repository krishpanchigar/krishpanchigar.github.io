Natural Language Processing - Sentence Parsing with Context-Free Grammar

In the realm of Natural Language Processing (NLP), understanding sentence structure is key. Parsing sentences enables computers to comprehend their meaning and extract valuable information. In this project, we employ context-free grammar to parse English sentences and unveil their structure. It's a fascinating journey into the world of NLP and context-free grammars.
